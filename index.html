Reading assistant

Vijñāna Bhairava Tantra

विज्ञान भैरव तन्त्र

Meant as an assistant while you read the book — hear the sound of each śloka alongside its meaning, so the verse can settle as both voice and sense.

Use this beside your printed book: listen to each śloka, follow the Devanagari, and keep the sense close at hand. Chanted audio will be wired in once rendering is complete. See NOTICE for acknowledgments.
