Vijñāna Bhairava Tantra

विज्ञान भैरव तन्त्र

Meant as an assistant while you read the book — hear the sound of each śloka alongside its meaning, so the verse can settle as both voice and sense.

Listen to all 163 numbered verses, plus the separately transmitted breath-mantra stanza 156-A. The English layer offers brief orientation rather than replacing a critical translation or commentary. See NOTICE for provenance and acknowledgments.
